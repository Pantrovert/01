# **App Name**: JourneyLens Portfolio

## Core Features:

- Personalized Introduction: A dynamic landing page offering a concise overview of the portfolio owner's unique professional identity.
- Academic Milestones: Showcase of educational background, including degrees, institutions, and key academic achievements.
- Professional Trajectory: A chronological display of work experience, roles, responsibilities, and significant contributions.
- Skills & Competencies: A categorized presentation of technical proficiencies, soft skills, and areas of expertise.
- Project Portfolio: Detailed showcasing of personal and professional projects, complete with descriptions, technologies used, and external links.
- AI-Powered 'Ask Me Anything' Tool: An interactive chatbot allowing visitors to query the portfolio content directly, with the AI tool generating relevant, contextualized responses about the owner's journey, skills, and projects.
- Direct Contact Form: A user-friendly form for visitors to submit inquiries or collaboration proposals.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep, professional blue (#264C73), conveying reliability and expertise, suited for a light theme.
- Background color: A very light, subtly desaturated blue-grey (#ECEFF2), providing a clean and understated canvas.
- Accent color: A vibrant, clear cyan (#47C3E1), used for highlights, interactive elements, and to introduce a touch of modernity and energy.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif), for a contemporary and technically informed aesthetic.
- Body text font: 'Inter' (sans-serif), ensuring excellent readability for longer descriptions and textual content.
- Utilize minimalist, line-art icons that complement the professional and clean aesthetic, clearly representing each section and interaction.
- Implement a modular and responsive layout, ensuring a seamless experience across all devices, with clear hierarchy for content sections.
- Incorporate subtle and elegant animations for page transitions and element reveals, enhancing user engagement without distraction.
